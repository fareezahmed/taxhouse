Depending upon the nature of the business, you may opt for business registerations. We help you get the Australian Business Number (ABN), AUSkey and tax registrations.


  • ABN registration for individuals (sole traders).
  • ABN registration for companies, partnerships, trust and other organisations.
  • Add a new business account.

GST:

We help you register for goods and services tax (GST) if you run a business or non-profit organisation based upon the GST turnover threshold.


PAYG:

We help you with registeration for Pay as you go (PAYG) withholding. You may need to register for fringe benefits tax if you provide fringe benefits. You will have to pay super for your eligible employees but you don't need to register for super.


Fringe Benefits Tax:

We recommend you to register for Fringe benefits tax as soon as you decide to provide benefits to your employees.

As a registered BAS agent we provide business activity statement services (BAS services) such as giving taxpayers advice about their obligations under a BAS provision, preparing and lodging approved forms about a taxpayer's liabilities, obligations or entitlements under a BAS provision, transacting with the ATO on behalf of a taxpayer in relation to the taxpayer's obligations under a BAS provision.


As a business you use an activity statement to:


  • Report and Pay the GST, your business has collected.
  • Claim GST Credits.

We prepare BAS personalised to each business or individual. We make activity statements to report and pay a number of tax obligations, including GST, pay as you go (PAYG) instalments, PAYG withholding and fringe benefits tax. We also help individuals who need to pay quarterly PAYG instalments also use activity statements. We help you lodge BAS on time before the due date to avaoid interest and penalties.

We look after your invoicing, paying and record-keeping. Some of common business activities, we perform are as follows:


  • issuing invoices and paying your own bills (sole traders).
  • making wages and super payments
  • preparing cash books (paper or electronic)
  • preparing activity statements
  • preparing tax returns
  • preparing financial accounts – for example, profit and loss statements and balance sheets.

We provide a range of accounting services for our clients and take the responsibility for tasks and functions such as:


  • End to End Payroll

  • Accounts Payable and Receivable

We keep your business records by law generally for a period of five years after they are prepared, obtained or the transaction is completed, whichever occurs latest and in a form that we can access and understand in order to work out the tax our clients are liable to pay.


By keeping your records we help you achieve the following:

  • Complete activity statements, income tax returns and fringe benefits tax returns.
  • Provide written evidence of tax related transactions.
  • Reduce the risk of tax audits and adjustments.
  • Improve communication with ATO.
  • Resolve issues relating to disputed assessments or adjustments.
  • Avoid exposure to penalties.

Some records may need to be kept for longer periods, such as records relating to capital gains tax events. However, shorter record keeping periods apply for some records for individual clients with simple tax affairs.
